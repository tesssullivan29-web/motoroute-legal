2 Description of Service

MotoPath is a motorcycle routing and ride companion application that provides the following features:

  • Route discovery based on ride type (calm, fast, long, short, sightseeing, city, country)
  • Real-time GPS navigation and turn-by-turn directions
  • Live weather conditions and riding score
  • Ride sharing and group ride coordination
  • Rider achievement and progress tracking
  • Favorite route saving and ride history

The App is available on iOS devices through the Apple App Store. A free tier and a premium subscription ("MotoPath Pro") are offered.
