3. We Do Not Sell Your Data

MotoPath does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

4. Location Data

MotoPath requests access to your device location to display routes near you and to track your ride in real time. Location data is processed on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. Background location access is used solely during active navigation sessions.

6. Data Retention

All ride data and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete All My Data" option in Settings → Privacy & Data.

7. Children's Privacy

MotoPath is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
